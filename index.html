xdg application menu with full support for the additional categories for fvwm and other xdg compliant desktops

Welcome to Fvwm-xdg-menu

Fvwm-Xdg-menu is a xdg application menu with full support for the additional categories. It is designed with fvwm in mind but should work with any xdg compliant desktop.

Well almost, it doesn't work with kde. Please don't fill a bug report here, but complain to the kde team instead. Their menu implementation doesn't support fully the additional categories, is not compatible with a compliant menu like this one, and that's definitely not my problem.
